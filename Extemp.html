11:00am-12:00pm Introduction & AGD Incorporation
  • Students will be given a set of topics (economics topics) by which then can use the jokes from the AGD work lecture to craft effective introductions

  • This lecture will look at the effective methods necessary for creating successful context sections within an introduction

Substructure & Body-Structure Tips
Time Activity
9:00-9:45am U.L.I Substructure
  • Here, students will learn about substructure related to What questions and questions based on change
9:45-10:00am Break
10:00-10:45am U.L.I Work
  • Students will give various one-and-outs where they become proficient on foreign policy questions as they relate to U.L.I questions
10:45-11:00am Break
11:00am-12:00pm Dash-Drill & 1-and-Out
  • Students will learn about the dash drill and think about creative ways that they can off-hand make warrants for their uniqueness, link, and impact arguments
  • Students will use this information to make 1-and-outs
  • They will need to be proficient on the body structure
12:00-1:00pm Lunch
1:00-1:30pm P.S.I Substructure
  • Here, students will learn about substructure related to Why, How, and Should questions and questions based on change
1:30-1:40pm Break
1:40-2:40pm Foreign Policy Lecture
  • Here, the students will be given information as it relates to foreign policy that they can universally port into various speeches
2:40-2:50pm Break
2:50-3:30pm 2-and-Out
  • Students will be given 15 minutes to prepare various 2-and-out under pressure on foreign policy related questions.
3:30-3:40pm Break
3:40-4:15pm Link Analysis Tips
  • Students will be given tips of how they can methodically think through the link analysis, and structural components that will help make their link analysis fluid.
4:15-4:30pm Break
4:30-5:30pm Link Analysis Drills
  • Students will have 10 minutes to think through their link analysis, develop it, and then present it in a manner that is conducive for both presentation and content.
5:30-6:00pm Q&A
  • We will breakdown the questions and answers that we should do
