Janet Melton and Sandra Greene Speech and Debate Institute!

A four day boot camp in speech, debate, and journalism competitions. This camp was named in honor of two outstanding women that served Central Texas and the greater Speech and Debate community.
July 18th to July 21st at Salado High School
Program Options

CX/LD Debate

Novice and varsity divisions featuring small class sizes that will help your student develop the skills and knowledge needed to find success.

Extemp/Congress

A practical approach to speechmaking that places emphasis on the latest trends and techniques in a variety of competition circuits.

Prose/Poetry

A comprehensive analysis of the entire process of cultivating an oral interpretation performance. Students will learn how to select, cut, and perform WINNING pieces.

Journalism

Hyper focused instruction on the fundamentals and advanced techniques required to find success in News, Feature, Editorial, Headline, and Copy Editing.

Journalism

Coaching Clinic

A comprehensive overview of the different rules and strategies of each of the events featured in this camp. Additional instruction on the skills needed to find success in the classroom like lesson planning, managing a budget, building a tournament schedule, and professional networking! CPE credit will be provided.

Cost of Attendance
At just $450.00 per student, our program gives students an unrivaled opportunity to experience all of the fun of a camp for less than half of the fees!
Entry fees cover the cost of meals, faculty contracts, facility maintenance, and any other materials that the students might leave with!
The cost of lodging is not included in your entry fees, but we have partnered with several hotels in the area to secure discounted rates for the week.
Lodging
